Cyber Flora

Category
Art Direction
Year
2024
Role
Creative Director

Cyber Flora is an art direction project for a digital nature exhibition that explores the boundary between organic growth and computational generation.

The visual identity merges botanical illustration traditions with glitch aesthetics, creating a world where plants exist as data structures and pixels bloom like flowers.

The exhibition featured interactive installations where visitors could influence the growth patterns of digital organisms through movement and sound.
