Synth Wave

Category
Illustration
Year
2024
Role
Artist

Synth Wave is a personal illustration series exploring themes of retro-futurism, digital nostalgia, and the aesthetics of 80s anime.

Each piece combines hand-drawn linework with digital coloring techniques, creating a hybrid style that feels both analog and synthetic. The palette draws from sunset gradients and CRT color bleeding.

The series has been featured in various online galleries and print publications focused on contemporary digital art.
