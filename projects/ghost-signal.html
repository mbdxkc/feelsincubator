Ghost Signal

Category
Motion
Year
2024
Role
Motion Designer

Ghost Signal is a motion design project exploring themes of digital ephemera, signal decay, and the aesthetics of broken transmissions.

The work uses procedural animation techniques combined with hand-crafted keyframes to create organic movement that feels simultaneously controlled and chaotic.

Sound design plays a crucial role, with audio-reactive elements driving visual changes. The project was created for an audiovisual installation exhibited in Tokyo.
