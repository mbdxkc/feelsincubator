Neon Dreams

Category
Brand Identity
Year
2024
Role
Design Lead

Neon Dreams is a brand identity project that explores the intersection of cyberpunk aesthetics and contemporary design language. The visual system draws from underground anime culture, late-night cityscapes, and digital artifacts.

The identity centers around a dynamic color palette of electric cyan and magenta, paired with stark black backgrounds. Typography choices emphasize geometric precision with subtle warmth, creating a balance between cold digital spaces and human expression.

The system extends across digital and print touchpoints, maintaining coherence while allowing for expressive variation. Each application reinforces the core tension between organic and synthetic.

Deliverables included logo system, color specifications, typography guidelines, iconography, social media templates, and a comprehensive brand book documenting usage across contexts.
